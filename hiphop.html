Hip Hop Dance

Energetic street dance with freestyle expression

About Hip Hop

Hip Hop is a highly energetic street dance style that emphasizes rhythm, freestyle movement, and creative expression. Originating in NYC, it has become a global cultural phenomenon.

Key Moves

Popping, locking, breaking, and freestyle improvisation are the core moves. Battles showcase style and creativity.
